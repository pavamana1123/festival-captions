#Narasimah Astottara Song

nārasiṁha mahāsiṁha divya siṁha mahābalah |
~ugra siṁha mahā deva staṁbajaś cogra locana ||

raudra sarvādbhuta śrīmān yogānandas trivikramaḥ |
~hariṁ kolahalaś cākri vijayo jaya vardhana ||

pancānanaḥ paraṁ brahma cāghoro ghora vikramaḥ |
~jvalan mukho jvalamāli mahā jvalo mahā prabhuḥ ||

niṭilākṣa sahasrākṣo durnirikṣyaḥ parantapaḥ |
~mahā daṁṣṭra yudhah prajṣāś canḍakopi sadāśivaḥ ||

hiranyaka-nisūdana daitya dānava bhaṣjanaḥ |
~guṇa bhadro maha bhadro bala-bhadraḥ subhadrakaḥ ||

karālo vikarālaś ca vikarta sarva-katrika |
~śiṣumāras trilokātma īsaḥ sarveśvara-vibhuḥ ||

bhairavā-ḍaṁbharo divyaś cācyutaḥ kavi mādhavaḥ |
~adhokśajo 'kśaraḥ sarvo vana-māli vara-pradaḥ ||

viśvaṁbharo 'dbhuto bhavyaḥ śrī-viṣṇuḥ puruṣottamaḥ |
~anaṣgastro nakṣatraś ca suryo jyotir sureśvaraḥ ||

sahasra bāhuḥ sarvajṣaḥ sarva-siddhi pradāyakaḥ |
~vajra-daṁṣṭro vajra-nakho mahānanda paraṁ-tapaḥ ||

sarva-mantraikarūpaś ca sarva-yantra vidāraṇaḥ |
~sarva tantra mahāvyaktaḥ suvyakto bhakta-vatsalaḥ ||

vaiṣākha-śukla bhutoltha śaraṇāgata-vatsalaḥ |
~udāra-kīrtiḥ punyātma mahātma canḍa-vikramaḥ ||

vatatrayo prapūjyas ca bhagavān parameśvaraḥ |
~śrī-vatsāngaḥ śrī-nivāso jagad-vyāpijaganmayaḥ ||

jagat pālo jagannātho mahākāyo dvirupabṛt |
~paramātma paraṁ-jyoti nirguṇaś ca nṛ-kesari ||

para-tattvaṁ paraṁ-dhāma sac-cid-ānanda-vigraḥ |
~lakṣmī nṛsiṁha sarvātma dhīra prahlāda-pālakaḥ ||

nārasiṁha mahāsiṁha divya siṁha mahābalah |
~ugra siṁha mahā deva staṁbajaś cogra locana ||
